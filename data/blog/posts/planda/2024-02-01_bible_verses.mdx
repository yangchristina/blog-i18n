A planner with Bible verses is a great way to keep your faith in mind as you go about your day. Whenever you feel like you need a little extra encouragement, you can click on Plandy, our panda mascot, and be reminded of God's love for you.

![image](/blog/mascot-bible-verse.png)

#### Instructions

Navigate to your settings, and check 'biblical' under 'Quote settings'. If you have both 'inspirational' and 'biblical' checked, you will see a random quote from either category.

![image](/blog/planda-settings.png)
