### Share your Calendar

Go to [https://planda.ca/social/share](https://planda.ca/socials/share) to generate a link to your calendar. You can share this link with anyone, and they will be able to view your calendar without needing to sign in.

Make sure you only share this link with people you trust, as anyone with the link will be able to view your calendar.

People with the link will not be able to make changes to your calendar.

![image](/blog/planda-share.png)


### Hide details of your events

We recommend you hide the details of your events when sharing your calendar, to protect your privacy.
