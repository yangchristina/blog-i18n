Want our mail panda to deliver messages to your friends?

![image](/blog/mail/find.png)

Send a friend request to your friends.

![image](/blog/mail/friend_req.png)

Once your friend accepts your friend request, you can send messages to your friends.

Once a message is read, it disappears forever.
